The core mental model

“Free” moves the bill somewhere else.

Zapier grew from a 2011 idea into the best-known hosted no-code automation brand.[S1] Its alternatives reduce subscription cost by adding usage caps, infrastructure work, or a narrower job.

Category mission status

No single official mission exists for “free Zapier alternatives.”

Inferred direction: make disconnected software act like one system without forcing every user to write and operate bespoke integration code.Each alternative trades managed convenience for a different mix of limits, control, and technical labor.
1

Free hosted tier

The vendor runs it, but credits, active flows, accounts, or advanced features are capped.

2

Free self-hosted

The software is free; your computer, server, backups, updates, and uptime are the cost.

3

Free desktop RPA

Your PC performs clicks and keystrokes. Useful locally, but it is not always-on cloud automation.

4

Free but tiny

Runs may be unlimited while the number or complexity of automations is severely restricted.

Decision path

Choose by where the workflow runs.

Self-hosted

Code is the product

Developer platform

Use Windmill for scripts and internal tools; Node-RED for event-driven and edge systems.

The task lives on a PC

Desktop actions

Use Power Automate Desktop. For only two simple links, IFTTT may be enough.
